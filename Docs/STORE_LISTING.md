## Full Description
Breakout+ is a fast, modern brick-breaker built for smooth 60+ FPS play and big screens. Smash through themed levels, chain combos, and stack powerups while the neon HUD keeps you locked in.

Modes:
- Classic: clear every level with steady difficulty ramps.
- Timed: race the clock for the highest score.
- Endless: survive as the pace escalates.
- Rush: higher speeds and aggressive scoring.
- Survival: one life with faster speed ramps.
- God Mode: relaxed play with no lives lost.
- Invaders: moving alien fleets fire back while your shield absorbs hits.

Powerups:
- Multi-Ball, Laser, Guardrail, Shield, Wide Paddle, Shrink
- Slow Motion, Overdrive, Fireball, Magnet, Gravity Well
- Ball Splitter, Freeze, Pierce, Extra Life

Brick types:
- Reinforced, Armored, Explosive, Unbreakable
- Moving, Spawning, Phase, Boss, Invader

Highlights:
- Daily challenges and combo-based scoring
- Foldable-friendly layout with high refresh rate support
- Crisp touch controls with smooth aim guide, responsive paddle feel, FIRE button for lasers, and polished effects

Whether you want a chill run or a high-speed score chase, Breakout+ delivers the classic feel with modern performance and style.
